<p>The <b>Manbij offensive</b>, code-named <b>Operation Martyr and Commander Faysal Abu Layla</b> by the SDF, was a 2016 <a href="/wiki/Offensive_(military)" title="Offensive (military)">military offensive operation</a> by the <a href="/wiki/Syrian_Democratic_Forces" title="Syrian Democratic Forces">Syrian Democratic Forces</a> (SDF) to capture the city of <a href="/wiki/Manbij" title="Manbij">Manbij</a> from the <a href="/wiki/Islamic_State_of_Iraq_and_the_Levant" title="Islamic State of Iraq and the Levant">Islamic State of Iraq and the Levant</a> (ISIL), and eventually, the ISIL-held areas through <a href="/wiki/Al-Bab" title="Al-Bab">Al-Bab</a> to <a href="/wiki/Herbel" title="Herbel">Herbel</a>, in the area referred to as the "Manbij Pocket"<sup id="cite_ref-Manbij_Pocket_78-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Manbij_Pocket-78">[78]</a></sup> in the northern <a href="/wiki/Aleppo_Governorate" title="Aleppo Governorate">Aleppo Governorate</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-link_all_cantons_79-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-link_all_cantons-79">[79]</a></sup> The main goal of the offensive was to cut off ISIL's last supply routes from <a href="/wiki/Turkey" title="Turkey">Turkey</a>, and to prevent ISIL fighters from escaping across the border.<sup id="cite_ref-launch_offensive_57-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-launch_offensive-57">[57]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Fallujah_and_Manbij_80-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Fallujah_and_Manbij-80">[80]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Manbij_used_to_plot_attacks_81-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Manbij_used_to_plot_attacks-81">[81]</a></sup> During the first 5 days of the offensive, the <a href="/wiki/US-led_coalition" class="mw-redirect" title="US-led coalition">US-led coalition</a> conducted over 55 airstrikes in support of the SDF.<sup id="cite_ref-Fallujah_and_Manbij_80-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Fallujah_and_Manbij-80">[80]</a></sup> After capturing Manbij city on 12 August, the SDF announced that the offensive would continue until the whole countryside around Manbij was captured,<sup id="cite_ref-continue_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-continue-1">[1]</a></sup> though the offensive effectively ended shortly after Turkish-backed forces started <a href="/wiki/Turkish_military_intervention_in_Syria" title="Turkish military intervention in Syria">Operation Euphrates Shield</a>.</p>
