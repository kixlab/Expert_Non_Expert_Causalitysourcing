<p>In late December 2015, the SDF captured the <a href="/wiki/Tishrin_Dam" title="Tishrin Dam">Tishrin Dam</a> and crossed the <a href="/wiki/Euphrates" title="Euphrates">Euphrates</a>, capturing the <a href="/wiki/Tishrin,_Aleppo" title="Tishrin, Aleppo">town of Tishrin</a> and other nearby areas from ISIL, paving the way for a future offensive toward Manbij.</p>
